About Me

I am Luis, a researcher in Natural Language Processing and Computational Creativity, specifically in written language.

My doctoral studies have focused on semantic literary and conceptual analysis through neural architectures for the deep analysis of linguistic corpora. The aim is to develop models for Automatic Text Generation, Information Retrieval, sentiment analysis, personality analysis, among others.
